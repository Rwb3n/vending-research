## 1. CROSSWAYS BUSINESS PARK / COMMERCIAL PARK
**Location:** Galleon Boulevard / Crossways Commercial Way, Dartford, Kent DA2 6QD/6QS
**M25 Junction:** J1a (direct access)
**Size:** 300-acre employment park. Planning consent for 3+ million sq ft of offices, warehousing and industrial. ~85% built out. Phase 2 adding 318,000 sq ft (3 units: 65,815 / 124,785 / 127,840 sq ft).
**Businesses:** ~45 companies registered in DA2 6NW postcode alone. Major tenants include:
- John Lewis, Kuehne+Nagel, ASDA, Sainsbury's (distribution)
- Europa, DHL, Yodel (logistics)
- Albion Fine Foods, Mission Produce (food distribution)
- Diebold Nixdorf, Custodian Data Centres
- Goodman (developer/landlord for commercial park section)
**Business types:** Predominantly logistics, distribution, data centres, food distribution
**Nearest food retail:** Dartford town centre ~3 miles. Limited on-site retail. Crossways Retail Park nearby but across major roads.
**Accessibility:** Excellent — direct M25 J1a access. Large loading yards. HGV-friendly.
**Vending opportunity:** HIGH. Large logistics workforce, 24/7 shift patterns, limited walkable food options on the estate itself. 500+ workers easily.

## 2. DP WORLD LONDON GATEWAY LOGISTICS PARK
**Location:** The Manorway, Stanford-le-Hope, Thurrock, Essex SS17
**M25 Junction:** Via A13 (J30/31 M25)
**Size:** 1,500 acres total (former Shell Haven site). 9 million sq ft logistics park. ~50% developed. 17 facilities built under first LDO. New 10-year expansion approval granted 2025.
**Businesses/Tenants:**
- UPS (32,000 sqm package sorting facility)
- DHL, Cosco, Ziegler, Curry's
- PAV Haulage (94,000 sq ft, 20-year lease)
- OASIS Group
- Destiny Entertainments, Ranson (2025 arrivals)
**Business types:** Port logistics, distribution, sorting/fulfilment
**Nearest food retail:** Stanford-le-Hope ~2 miles. Very isolated site. Freeport status.
**Accessibility:** Purpose-built logistics park. Full HGV infrastructure. Rail-served.
**Vending opportunity:** VERY HIGH. Extremely isolated. 24/7 port operations. Growing workforce. Workers are truly captive — nothing walkable nearby.

## 6. MANOR ROYAL BUSINESS DISTRICT
**Location:** Crawley, West Sussex RH10
**M25 Junction:** Via M23 J10
**Size:** 540 acres. 9 million sq ft commercial floorspace. 700 business units. UK's largest industrial BID.
**Workers:** 30,000+ employees. 18,000 commute in daily from outside Crawley.
**Businesses:** 600+ businesses including:
- Virgin Atlantic (HQ)
- Thales UK
- Tesco.com
- Elekta, Varian Medical Systems
- Deloitte, Kreston Reeves
- Nestle
- Amey, CGG, Vent Axia, Metrobus
- B&CE
**Business types:** Hugely diverse — aviation, medical tech, distribution, professional services, manufacturing, food production, printing
**GVA:** £1.7 billion annually
**Nearest food retail:** Bank Precinct within Manor Royal has some amenities. Crawley town centre ~1 mile. Some on-site cafes but vast majority of estate is food-desert for workers.
**Accessibility:** M23 J10. Good road network. Bus routes. Three Bridges station nearby.
**Vending opportunity:** VERY HIGH. 30,000 workers across 540 acres. Despite some on-site provision, the sheer scale means huge areas are underserved. Multiple building clusters are 10+ minute walk from any food outlet. This is the single best target in the research.

**Size:** 116 hectares (286 acres). Second largest industrial estate in London (after Park Royal).
**Workers:** ~7,500 employees across 285 businesses
**GVA:** £721 million annually
**Key tenants:**
- Johnson Matthey (chemicals/precious metals)
- Warburtons (bakery)
- Greggs (bakery distribution)
- DHL (logistics)
- UOP
- Enfield Power Station (adjacent)
**Sub-estates:** Watermill Business Centre, Sovereign Business Centre, Leeside Business Centre, Riverwalk Business Park, Delta Industrial Park
**Business types:** Manufacturing, bakery/food production, logistics, chemicals, distribution, SME units

**Vending opportunity:** VERY HIGH. 7,500 workers on a 286-acre site with minimal food retail. Mix of large manufacturers (shift workers) and SME units. Multiple sub-estates each need coverage.

## PRIORITY RANKING FOR VENDING TERRITORY

### Tier 1 — Highest Priority (captive workforce, 500+ workers, isolated)
1. **Manor Royal, Crawley** — 30,000 workers, 540 acres, UK's largest industrial BID. Multiple placement opportunities.
2. **Brimsdown, Enfield** — 7,500 workers, 286 acres, London's 2nd largest industrial estate. Very underserved for food.
3. **DP World London Gateway, Thurrock** — Growing port logistics park. Extremely isolated. 24/7 operations.
4. **Crossways Business Park, Dartford** — 300 acres, major logistics tenants. Limited on-site food.

### Tier 2 — Strong Opportunities
5. **Purley Way Industrial Pockets** (Beddington TE, Valley IP) — Target the industrial estates, not the retail strip.
6. **Thames Road / Erith-Belvedere corridor** — Dense industrial strip, limited food.
7. **London Medway Commercial Park** — 115 acres, Amazon/Wincanton anchor.
8. **Titan Works, Grays** — 35 acres, 28 new units, growing.

### Tier 3 — Secondary
9. **Salfords / Gatwick Business Park / North Gatwick Gateway** — Smaller, fragmented.
10. **Riverside Industrial Estate, Dartford** — Small but underserved.
11. **Globe Industrial Estate, Grays** — SME-focused.

### Avoid
- **West Thurrock / Lakeside area** — Too close to Lakeside Shopping Centre food court. Workers not captive.
