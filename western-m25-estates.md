# Western M25 Corridor — Business Parks & Industrial Estates
## Vending Machine Territory Planning Research
### Date: 2026-03-22

---

## 1. PARK ROYAL INDUSTRIAL ESTATE (TOP PRIORITY)

- **Location:** NW10 / W3, London Boroughs of Ealing & Brent
- **Postcode area:** NW10, W3, W5
- **Size:** ~584 hectares (~1,443 acres). Largest industrial estate in London.
- **Businesses:** 1,700–2,000+ businesses
- **Workers:** 36,000–52,000+ jobs (sources vary; GLA study says 52,300; Park Royal Business Group says 40,000+)
- **Units:** Hundreds of individual units across dozens of sub-estates
- **Business types:** Food manufacturing (produces ~1/3 of London's food — 500+ food manufacturers), logistics/distribution, light industrial, creative industries, automotive. Major food brands: Diageo, Coca-Cola. Also: Sky, Disney, Cisco, British Airways, SEGRO properties.
- **Nearest food retail:** Dispersed within the area. Some streets have virtually nothing. Many workers rely on mobile catering vans. The GLA strategy document notes services are "dispersed within the area which weakens its role."
- **Accessibility:** Good road access (A40, North Circular A406). Parking varies by sub-estate. Loading bays standard on industrial units.
- **Vending opportunity:** EXTREMELY HIGH. Massive captive workforce, especially in food manufacturing (shift workers), logistics warehouses, and smaller industrial units where there is no canteen. The sheer scale and fragmented ownership means many pockets are underserved.

## 2. SLOUGH TRADING ESTATE

- **Location:** Slough, Berkshire
- **Postcode area:** SL1
- **Size:** 353 acres
- **Businesses:** ~350 firms operating from ~435 units
- **Workers:** ~13,000 (per BBC centenary report); some sources say up to 10,000–13,000
- **Business types:** Mixed — logistics, offices, light manufacturing, R&D, data centres. Owner is SEGRO. Transactions range from 500 sq ft startup units to 45,000 sq ft facilities.
- **Notable tenants:** Mars, Dulux (AkzoNobel), various logistics operators. Historically: Citroen, Black & Decker.
- **Nearest food retail:** Some on-estate catering exists but many areas of the estate are remote from shops. The estate is large enough that workers in warehouse/logistics units on the periphery have limited walkable options.
- **Accessibility:** Excellent — adjacent to M4 (J6/J7), close to M25 (J15). Dedicated estate roads. Parking and loading standard.
- **Vending opportunity:** HIGH. 13,000 captive workers across a sprawling site. Warehouse and logistics workers are the primary target — shift patterns mean they need 24/7 refreshment options.

## 3. MANOR ROYAL, CRAWLEY (GATWICK CORRIDOR)

- **Location:** Crawley, West Sussex (north of town, near Gatwick Airport)
- **Postcode area:** RH10
- **Size:** ~240 hectares (~593 acres); 9,000,000 sq ft of commercial space
- **Businesses:** 600+ businesses
- **Workers:** 30,000+ jobs
- **Units:** 700 business units
- **Business types:** Aviation/airline services, advanced manufacturing, logistics, offices, pharma. Mix of large HQ buildings and smaller industrial/warehouse units.
- **Notable tenants:** Virgin Atlantic, Thales, Nestle, Elekta, CGG
- **Nearest food retail:** Some on-estate food options exist but the BID (Manor Royal Business Improvement District — mbid26.org) area is large. Many peripheral units are food-desert zones.
- **Accessibility:** Adjacent to M23, close to M25 (J7). Good road layout, standard loading/parking.
- **Vending opportunity:** HIGH. 30,000 workers across 700 units. The BID structure means there is an organised management body to approach. Large shift-working logistics operations near Gatwick are prime targets.

**Note:** Manor Royal is south of the M25, not technically on the western corridor (Heathrow-to-Watford). Including it because it is close to M25 J7 and is a major estate worth considering.

## 4. STOCKLEY PARK, UXBRIDGE

- **Location:** Uxbridge, London Borough of Hillingdon
- **Postcode area:** UB11
- **Size:** 150 acres (416 acres total site including parkland); 25 buildings; 165,000 sqm (~1.78 million sq ft) of office space. ULI reports 2 million+ sq ft built.
- **Businesses:** 20+ major occupiers (campus-style, large buildings)
- **Workers:** Data not confirmed — estimate several thousand based on building sizes
- **Business types:** Corporate HQ / office park. Tech, pharma, professional services.
- **Notable tenants:** Apple, GSK, Canon, Hasbro, Samsonite, Toshiba, Gilead, Marks & Spencer, Fiserv, Celgene, MSC
- **Nearest food retail:** On-park amenities exist (The Square has food/retail). This is a premium managed campus, so some catering is provided.
- **Accessibility:** Close to M4 (J4), M25 (J15), Heathrow. 88 acres of green space. Well-managed parking.
- **Vending opportunity:** MODERATE. This is a premium office campus — many buildings likely have their own catering/kitchens. Opportunity may be in lobbies, breakout areas, or buildings without full canteens. The corporate tenants may require premium/healthy vending.

## 5. SEGRO PARK GREENFORD CENTRAL

- **Location:** Greenford, London Borough of Ealing
- **Postcode area:** UB6
- **Size:** 250,000+ sq ft of modern industrial space (with major redevelopment coming 2027/2028 adding more). Multiple units (A through P+ identified in listings).
- **Businesses:** Data not confirmed for exact count — multi-unit estate with individual warehouses
- **Workers:** Data not confirmed
- **Business types:** Industrial, warehouse, trade counter, distribution
- **Notable tenants:** SEGRO-managed. Various SME and mid-size logistics/trade occupiers.
- **Nearest food retail:** Greenford has some high street retail nearby but the estate itself is industrial with limited food options.
- **Accessibility:** Good — near A40, reasonable access to M25 via A40/M40. Standard industrial loading/parking.
- **Vending opportunity:** MODERATE-HIGH. Industrial/warehouse workers with limited food options. The redevelopment will bring new units and potentially more workers. Smaller estate than Park Royal but concentrated.

## 6. CROXLEY PARK, WATFORD

- **Location:** Watford, Hertfordshire
- **Postcode area:** WD18
- **Size:** 700,000 sq ft of office/business space across 13 buildings
- **Businesses:** Multiple tenants (campus-style)
- **Workers:** 2,400+ employees on site
- **Business types:** Corporate offices, HQ-style campus
- **Notable tenants:** Not confirmed from search results
- **Nearest food retail:** Managed campus with shuttle buses to Watford Junction (every 12 mins). Some on-site amenities. Not walkable to town centre.
- **Accessibility:** On M25 corridor. Free shuttle buses. Parking on site.
- **Vending opportunity:** MODERATE. 2,400 workers in a campus that is not walkable to shops. However, managed campus likely has some catering. Vending in lobbies/breakout areas could work. Worth investigating what on-site food exists.

## 7. WATFORD BUSINESS PARK

- **Location:** Watford, Hertfordshire (Greenhill Crescent / Caxton Way / Faraday Close area)
- **Postcode area:** WD18 / WD24
- **Size:** 26.3 hectares (~65 acres)
- **Businesses:** 100+ businesses
- **Workers:** Figure cut off in source PDFs — described as "employing approximately [X]" but the number was truncated. Likely 2,000–5,000 based on 100+ businesses.
- **Business types:** Mixed industrial/office. Major occupiers include Corona Energy, Kodak. New £25 million "Inspire" development (4.3 acres) recently completed.
- **Notable tenants:** Corona Energy, Kodak
- **Nearest food retail:** Some retail on periphery but the estate itself is industrial. Colonial Way area has logistics/warehouse units.
- **Accessibility:** Close to M1 (J5, 1.2 miles), M25 accessible. Standard industrial access.
- **Vending opportunity:** MODERATE-HIGH. 100+ businesses on a sizeable estate. The mix of logistics and office means varied workforce. New development bringing in more occupiers.

## 8. PROLOGIS PARK WEST LONDON, WEST DRAYTON

- **Location:** West Drayton, London Borough of Hillingdon
- **Postcode area:** UB7 / UB11
- **Size:** Multiple large units — DC1 through DC6. Individual units range from 78,780 sq ft to 195,720 sq ft. Total park likely 500,000+ sq ft.
- **Businesses:** Small number of large occupiers (big-box logistics)
- **Workers:** Data not confirmed — large logistics warehouses typically employ 100–500+ per unit
- **Business types:** Logistics, distribution, B8 warehousing
- **Nearest food retail:** 15-minute walk to West Drayton Station (some retail there). The park itself is pure logistics with no retail.
- **Accessibility:** Excellent — near M25 (J15), M4 (J4/J4b). 15m clear height warehouses. Major loading facilities.
- **Vending opportunity:** HIGH for individual unit placements. Large logistics warehouses with shift workers and no food retail on site. Approach individual occupiers. Each warehouse is a significant placement opportunity.

## 9. HEATHROW CORPORATE PARK, HOUNSLOW

- **Location:** Green Lane, Cranford, Hounslow
- **Postcode area:** TW4
- **Size:** Multi-unit estate — 50/50 warehouse and office buildings. Exact total sq ft not confirmed.
- **Businesses:** Multiple occupiers including data centre operations
- **Workers:** Data not confirmed
- **Business types:** Mixed warehouse/office, data centre
- **Nearest food retail:** Cranford has some local retail but the park itself is gated/secure with limited amenities.
- **Accessibility:** Near Heathrow, good road access. Secure estate with parking.
- **Vending opportunity:** MODERATE. Smaller estate but secure/gated environment means captive workforce. Data centre staff work shifts.
